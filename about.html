About Me

Accountant, Hiker, & Cat Lover

I was born and raised in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. I spent my entire childhood in the same place with my family. Therefore, I did not need to experience the real world until I decided to study abroad.

After graduating high school, I came to Canada to continue my study. It was a huge turning point of my life. I had to learn to take care of myself and adapt to unfamiliar lifestyle and culture. On the brighter note, I was able to make new friends, enhance my network and learned many new things. Three years later, I moved to America. This time, I was well – prepared because of prior experiences and I am excited for new journey. America has been wonderful. Many doors have opened up for me. I attended one of the leading college in Dallas, Texas, got to study my dream accounting major and had my first real job there.

Even though I need to work hard, I found that commitment is essential for everything, and the achievements are well worth every obstacle. And I can’t wait for the next chapters after getting my bachelor degree in accounting. With my education and efforts, I believe that I can overcome any difficulties and finish the job.

Hiking

I’ve growth my love for hiking since I moved to Vancouver, Canada. It is an effective way to exercise and relax. Hiking is for everybody. Depends on individual references, it can be laid back like a normal walk, or it can be advance for someone who prefer extreme sport. The best part is always the scenic view at the end of the trail. At least, that’s what keep me going and the view never disappointed me.

If you are tired of going to the gym and doing the same routine every workout, give hiking a try. I’m sure that you won’t be regret.

Travelling

Everybody loves to travel including me. However, my thought about travel is a little different. When I moved to different cities and spent a decent time there, I found that you cannot get to know the place in short period of time. Therefore, if I have chances, I would love to live in different cities and countries around the world to experience the life at its fullness.

In my opinion, travel can broaden your mind and knowledge in many ways. The world is so colorful and diverse, and I cannot wait for my next trip or maybe a new life at a new city.
