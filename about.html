About Me

Accountant, Hiker, & Cat Lover

Hey! My name’s Anna. I am a graphic designer and illustrator based in Denver, Colorado. I am passionate about design and illustration and I want to help you make your next project be beautiful.

I graduated from Iowa State University in Ames, Iowa with a B.F.A in graphic design. Since then I have worked as a freelancer multiple times, worked as a designer for HVAC interface systems, worked retail at Jo-ann fabrics, made 8 costumes (for myself, thank you very much, and the number continues growing), and been a professional superhero. I currently work as a designer for for a company in Denver called AmuseMints.

When I’m not doing art you can find me customizing dolls, sewing costumes, playing video games, watching anime, writing, reading, or hanging out with my cat.
